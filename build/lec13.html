Test-Driven Development

Summary

Organizing Python code in functions and files leads to importable modules that help us to glue together more complex programs.

Resources

A good overview of how test-driven development has impacted scientific computing is given by Test-Driven Development in HPC Science: A Case Study

Evidence of Student Learning

  • Students will clone a sample repository with a predefined module.
  • Students will write unit tests.
  • Students will reflect on their learning by completing their daily log.
