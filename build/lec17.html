SciPy and Sparse Systems

Summary

Last time, we used NumPy to construct and solve linear systems. Sometimes, however, such constructions are just too expensive, with respect to both computational effort and memory requirements. Here, we look at SciPy and its interface for defining sparse systems, i.e., systems whose matrix has a whole bunch of zeros.
